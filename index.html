How We Make Our Products

Our specialized ferret food is made with 100% farm raised, cruelty free meat and poultry. Prior to harvesting, our animals are provided with proper care, including adequate space, food, and water, ensuring they are healthy and content. Facilities are regularly inspected to comply with humane treatment standards, ensuring that the welfare of the animals is always prioritized. Immediately after harvesting, our chefs begin preparing the meat to be cleaned, trimmed, and freeze-dried. The speed of this process helps to keep as many nutrients and necessary fats in our end products as possible and also reduces waste.

About Us

As actual ferret owners, we know how difficult and tedious it can be to find specialized food for them. With thier stictly carnivorous diet, it's difficult to find a food brand that contains no harmful fillers, grains, or additives. Most of us just ended up buying high fat high protien cat food in hopes that the grains and fillers would not cause blockages or sickness to our ferrets. It was at that moment that we knew something had to change.

There's food for almost all types of animals specifically catered to their dietary needs. Especially since most are omnivorous and can eat fruit, veggies, meat, and dairy. That is when we decided to specialize in ferret food that we knew we could trust. Our freeze-dried food is made with 100% all natural, farm raised meats. We felt more at ease knowing that our ferrets were getting the proper protien, fats, and nutrients needed to stay healthy. No more checking the label to see how much grain, fruits, or harmful products it may contain. Since our products are made of 100% meat, any carnivorous furry friend can get in on the action. We just want what every loving ferret parent wants. That's to provide them with the best care possible for a long and healthy life.
